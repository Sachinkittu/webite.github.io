Vision

To contribute to society through pursuit of education, learning, and research at the highest international levels of excellence and to provide student-centric education and foster personal and intellectual growth to prepare students for productive careers, meaningful lives and responsible citizen in a global society.

Mission

1. Teaching and scholarship
2. Research and discovery
3. Engagement and public service
4. Economic development
5. Healthcare
6. Arts and culture
7. Global reach
8. Sports & Recreation

Objectives

1. To establish ultra-modern, state of the art facilities for education and training in Higher Education.
2. To develop educational principles and practices which are best suited to the needs of our country and state.
3. To provide sufficient impetus for high quality of education from highly competent faculties at each level.
4. To create higher levels of intellectual abilities.
5. To arrest & reverse Brain Drain from the state.

what our Vice Chancellor says

India’s improved system is education is cited as one of the main contributors to its economic improvement.While enrollment in higher education is only 14% of the total primary education,it is far behind in comparison to developed and other developing countries in world.
This percentage has to be improved at par with developed countries. Keeping in view to this fact many private sector institutions have come up to provide higher education. Gopal Narayan Singh University, Sasaram, a private University, is non-profit university in remote and naxal area of Bihar, which has been established to provide higher education to downtrodden, poor & helpless people in Bihar.
DR. M. L. VERMA
Vice-Chancellor
